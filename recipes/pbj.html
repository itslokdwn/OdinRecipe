Peanut Butter and Jelly

Need something easy to make? Try settling for a tasy peanut butter and jelly sandwhich.

Ingredients:

  1. Peanut Butter /li>
  2. Jelly
  3. Choice of bread

Instructions

  1. Spread:

    First lets spread the peanut butter evenly on one slice of bread. Continue to spread the jelly on the other slice of bread.

  2. Combine:

    Combine both slices of bread evenly.

  3. Enjoy!!!
