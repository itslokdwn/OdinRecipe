Fresh Golden Fries

Here is the perfect french fries recipe.Based on a method that has been known worldwide, these hot fries are so crispy they stay the way they are after they've gone cold.

Materials:

  1. Fresh Potatoes
  2. Bowl of Vinegar
  3. Salt
  4. Oil

Instructions

  1. Cut:

    First lets cut the potato into 1/4 " sticks. Thicker = less crispy. Thinner = not enough potato inside.

  2. Soak:

    Rinse the cut fries in some water for about 15 seconds. Then transfer to a bowl of cold tap water and splash with vinegar and salt. Bring to a boil on high heat then immediately turn to low once boiling. Cook for 10 minutes.

  3. Drain and Dry:

    Using a colander, drain the water and spread the potatoes out on a towel lined tray to steam dry for 5 minutes.

  4. Cook:

    Fill a large pot with about an inch and a half of oil. preheat the oil to 205 degrees celcius. add in small batches of fries and fry for 50 seconds. Remove fries and let cool for 30 minutes.
